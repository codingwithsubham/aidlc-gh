# Reviews & Ratings

**Long Description**: This epic will enable customers to provide feedback on purchased products by submitting reviews and ratings. It includes moderation functionalities for admins to manage the visibility of these reviews based on specific criteria.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- Customers can only submit reviews for orders that have been delivered.
- Admins can manage, moderate, and remove reviews as deemed necessary.

**Criticality**: Medium

**Dependency Priority**: Order Processing & Tracking
