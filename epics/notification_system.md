# Notification System

**Long Description**: This epic implements a notification system to keep both customers and sellers informed about order statuses, product lows, and reviews. Notifications will be sent via email, ensuring effective communication.

**Acceptance Criteria**:
- Automated notifications are sent to customers on order status updates.
- Sellers and admins receive relevant alerts for their activities.

**Criticality**: Medium

**Dependency Priority**: Order Processing & Tracking
